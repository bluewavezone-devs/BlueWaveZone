Blue Wave Zone Code of Conduct for Business Partners

Cape Town, January 25, 2023

1. Policy

The "Code of Conduct for Business Partners" is a policy explaining how anyone doing business with or on behalf of Blue Wave Zone is expected to behave. It is about "doing the right thing" in general â€“ following the law, acting honorably and treating others with respect.

2. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all business partners, suppliers, contractors, and other third parties who conduct business with or on behalf of Blue Wave Zone.

3. Our Expectations

  1. Legal Compliance: All business partners must comply with all applicable laws and regulations.
  2. Business Integrity: No form of bribery, corruption, or unethical business practice will be tolerated.
  3. Fair Business Practices: We expect fair dealing with all stakeholders including customers, suppliers, and competitors.
  4. Respect for People: We are committed to human rights and fair labor practices.
  5. Health and Safety: We expect our partners to maintain safe working conditions.
  6. Environmental Responsibility: We expect our partners to minimize environmental impact.

4. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any violations of this Code of Conduct, please report them to:

Email: admin@bluewavezone.co.za

5. Consequences of Non-Compliance

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in termination of the business relationship.
